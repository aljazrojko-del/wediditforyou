# Premium Upsell — Review Call Script

The 15-min review call is where Starter ($297) becomes Premium ($797). About
**30-40% of "starter buyers" take the upsell** when this script runs cleanly.

Run this script AFTER they've said "yes" to Starter on the call — never before.
Pre-empting the upsell on the first touch tanks Starter conversion.

---

## Opener — when they say "OK, I'll take the $297"

> the four things that turn this site into an actual lead machine, not just a

90% of buyers say "yeah, what is it?" — that's permission to pitch.

## What NOT to do on the upsell

- ❌ Don't pitch Premium before they've said yes to Starter
- ❌ Don't dilute the offer with "or we could just do half of Premium for $497"
- ❌ Don't apologize for pricing — say the number, pause, let them respond
- ❌ Don't argue about value — pivot to the math (1 extra job/mo = break-even)
- ❌ Don't extend a "no" — say "no problem, sending Starter link"
- ❌ Don't follow up Starter buyers with Premium pitches for 48 hrs — they'll feel sold to
- ❌ Don't take cash discount requests as a yes — those are tire-kickers

## What WORKS

- ✅ State the price, then **shut up** until they speak
- ✅ Use their name 2-3 times during the upsell
- ✅ Mirror objections back ("$500 is a lot?") — repeat, then respond
- ✅ Frame against the $2,600 stack value — every component has a number
- ✅ Anchor against agency pricing ("usually $1,200 separately")
- ✅ End every objection response with a question ("worth it?", "deal?")
